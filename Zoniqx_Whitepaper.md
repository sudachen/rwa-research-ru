# Zoniqx - Operating System for Tokenized Real-World Assets

## Overview

Zoniqx is the operating system and distribution rails for tokenized real-world assets (RWAs). Headquartered in Silicon Valley, Zoniqx helps institutions and asset owners tokenize and manage RWAs with full compliance, AI automation, and global reach.

## The Market Gap in Tokenization

### Problems Zoniqx Solves

1. **Compliance Complexity** - Navigating multi-jurisdiction regulations
2. **Technical Fragmentation** - Lack of standardization across platforms
3. **Distribution Challenges** - Difficulty reaching global markets
4. **Lifecycle Management** - Complex asset governance and updates

### Zoniqx Solution

Zoniqx provides:
- **Full compliance automation** - AI-powered regulatory adherence
- **Multi-chain deployment** - 16+ live networks
- **Global distribution** - Multi-jurisdiction support
- **End-to-end lifecycle management** - From issuance to redemption
