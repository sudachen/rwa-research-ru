# BitCow - Cattle Tokenization Platform

## Overview

BitCow is a digital token backed by a real productive asset: cattle. It's a blockchain-powered tool that democratizes access to livestock investment, allowing investors to participate in cattle breeding with fractional ownership.

## What is BitCow

BitCow represents:
- Digital token backed by real cattle
- Fractional ownership of livestock
- Investment in Argentine beef industry
- Blockchain-based asset tracking

## How It Works

### Tokenization Process

1. **Asset Selection** - Real cattle are selected and verified
2. **Tokenization** - Each animal is represented by digital tokens
3. **Fractional Ownership** - Investors can buy fractions (as little as 1/10th)
4. **Value Tracking** - Token value follows cattle market prices

### Valuation

BitCow value is determined by:
- Evolution of cattle prices in the market
- Increase in number of BitCows (breeding)
- Market demand
- Livestock productivity
