# Brickken - Enterprise Tokenization Platform

## Overview

Brickken is a decentralized platform and enterprise tokenization suite that focuses on asset tokenization, enabling businesses and individuals to create and manage Security Token Offerings (STOs) and tokenize real-world assets with No-Code solutions.

## What is Brickken

Brickken provides a comprehensive Digital Asset Suite for:
- Asset tokenization (equity, bonds, loans, real estate)
- Fundraising and capital raising
- Cross-border payments
- Efficient asset management
- Compliance automation
