# Noble - Native Asset Issuance for Cosmos

## Overview

Noble is an appchain purpose-built for native asset issuance in Cosmos and the boundless Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC) ecosystem. Noble serves as the official issuance platform for USDC and other native assets in the Cosmos ecosystem.

## What is Noble

Noble is a specialized blockchain designed specifically for:
- Native asset issuance
- Stablecoin infrastructure
- Cross-chain asset distribution
- IBC-native token standards

## USDC on Noble

### Native Cosmos USDC

USDC on Noble is the official form of USDC for the Cosmos ecosystem:
- Issued natively by Circle
- Not a wrapped or bridged token
- Full reserve backing
- Direct Circle support

### Key Benefits

1. **Native Issuance** - True native USDC, not wrapped
2. **IBC Integration** - Seamless transfers across Cosmos chains
3. **Simple & Safe** - Straightforward integration for developers
4. **Institutional Grade** - Circle's full backing and compliance
5. **Ecosystem Wide** - Available to all IBC-compatible chains
