# Latin America RWA Projects - Summary

This document provides an overview of blockchain and RWA tokenization projects in Latin America, focusing on Chile, Argentina, and Brazil.

## Overview

Latin American countries are pioneering real-world asset tokenization in sectors like agriculture, real estate, and SME financing. These projects address local market needs while leveraging blockchain technology.

## Projects by Country

### Argentina

#### Agrotoken
- **Focus**: Agricultural commodity tokenization
- **Assets**: Soybeans, corn, wheat
- **Status**: Active, expanding to Brazil
- **PDF**: Agrotoken_Whitepaper.pdf available

#### BitCow
- **Focus**: Cattle/livestock tokenization
- **Assets**: Live cattle
- **Status**: 2,000+ cattle tokenized, expanding to USA
- **Innovation**: Fractional cattle ownership

#### PALA Blockchain
- **Focus**: SME lending and tokenization platform
- **Services**: Asset tokenization infrastructure
- **Market**: Argentina, expanding regionally
- **Website**: https://www.palablockchain.io/

#### LITARSA
- **Focus**: Lithium reserves tokenization
- **Assets**: Lithium mining rights
- **Technology**: Cardano blockchain
- **Innovation**: First lithium tokenization project

#### Landtoken
- **Focus**: Agricultural land tokenization
- **Status**: First CNV-approved trust
- **Assets**: Farmland and agricultural properties

#### Enertoken / Justoken
- **Focus**: Electricity tokenization
- **Partner**: YPF Luz (major energy company)
- **Platform**: ENERTOKEN on XRP Ledger
- **Value**: $800M+ in tokenized PPAs

### Chile

#### Reity
- **Focus**: Real estate tokenization
- **Blockchain**: Algorand
- **Market**: First tokenized real estate marketplace in Chile
- **Innovation**: Property fractionalization

#### Fraccional.cl
- **Focus**: Real estate fractionalization
- **Structure**: SPAs and commercial code-based
- **Status**: Seeking CMF certification
- **Expansion**: Planning Spain expansion

#### RedCapital
- **Focus**: Invoice factoring and SME financing
- **Model**: Crowdfunding platform
- **Volume**: $90M+ processed, 3,800+ transactions
- **Investment**: $3M from HCS Capital

### Brazil

#### Agrotoken (Brazil operations)
- **Focus**: Agricultural commodity tokenization
- **Assets**: Soybeans, corn, wheat
- **Status**: Active operations in Brazil
- **Integration**: Cross-border with Argentina

#### Mercado Bitcoin Partnership
- **Partner**: Plume Network
- **Focus**: Bringing institutional RWA yield to Brazil
- **Innovation**: Nest Vaults integration

### Multi-Country

#### Dinari
- **Focus**: Tokenized stocks and bonds
- **Market**: Latin America access to US equities
- **Technology**: Multi-blockchain support
- **Status**: First broker-dealer registration for tokenized equities

## Regulatory Environment

### Argentina
- CNV (Comisión Nacional de Valores) oversight
- First approved tokenization trusts
- Growing regulatory clarity
- Innovation-friendly approach

### Chile
- CMF (Comisión para el Mercado Financiero) regulation
- Fintech Law framework
- Certification processes
- Conservative but evolving

### Brazil
- CVM (Comissão de Valores Mobiliários) oversight
- Growing institutional interest
- Partnership with major exchanges
- Expanding framework
