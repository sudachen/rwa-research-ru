# Kujira - Sustainable Sovereign Layer 1 Ecosystem

## Overview

Kujira is a decentralized ecosystem revolutionizing FinTech to provide sustainable solutions for web3 protocols, builders, and users. It's an innovation hub of revenue-generating products with great user experience, allowing retail investors to access opportunities previously only available to the wealthy and elite.

## The Kujira Blockchain

Kujira is a Layer 1 blockchain ecosystem that is:
- **Sovereign** - Independent and self-governing
- **Sustainable** - Long-term viable economic model
- **Decentralized** - Community-driven governance

## Vision

Kujira's mission is to build a range of easy-to-use, cost-effective financial tools that:
- Suit anyone: novice enthusiasts, seasoned retail investors, and institutions
- Allow income generation regardless of market direction
- Are inclusive of all blockchains and networks
- Democratize access to financial opportunities
